Where is my Refund?

When will you receive your refund? The answer depends on how you filed your return. If you chose to receive your refund through direct deposit, you should receive it within a week. If you use efile, your refund should be issued between two and three weeks. If filing a paper return, the IRS should issue your refund check within six to eight weeks.
